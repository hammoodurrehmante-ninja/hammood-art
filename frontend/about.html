I'm Hammood — a Lead Product Designer with 7 years of experience turning complex business problems into products people actually want to use. I've worked across SaaS, fintech, telecom, e-commerce, and enterprise — with startups, agencies, and global clients.

My strength is in making complexity feel simple. Whether it's a dense ERP system, a multi-tenant SaaS dashboard, or a mobile app — I focus on the user first and the pixels second. Good design isn't about aesthetics alone; it's about products that work the way people expect them to.

I currently work at Techesthete as Lead Product Designer, building AI-powered SaaS products for TikTok and Amazon advertising platforms. I also take on freelance work for global clients — designing everything from fintech apps to enterprise platforms to brand identities.

I apply AI-assisted design workflows daily — using tools like Claude, Figma AI, ChatGPT, and Figma Make to validate ideas faster, reduce iteration cycles, and ship better products sooner. AI doesn't replace the thinking — it accelerates it.
