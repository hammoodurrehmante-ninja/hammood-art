Project Overview
NextLevel is a digital real‑estate fintech platform designed to streamline the home‑buying journey by 
connecting home buyers, mortgage advisors, buying agents, and partner agencies within a single 
ecosystem. The platform simplifies complex processes such as mortgage eligibility checks, agent 
selection, portfolio management, and property bidding while providing transparency and real‑time 
progress tracking for all stakeholders.
